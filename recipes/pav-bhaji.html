Pav Bhaji

Description:

Pav Bhaji is a favorite dish and street food item for every hardcore Mumbaikar! Being a Mumbaikar, you always crave and love Pav Bhaji—the bhaji tastes delicious with a piece of melting butter on top, while the pav, literally dipped in butter, tastes absolutely awesome."

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes: 3 medium, boiled and peeled.
  • Green Peas: ½ cup, fresh or frozen.
  • Cauliflower: 1 cup, finely chopped.
  • Onions: 2 large, finely chopped.
  • Tomatoes: 3 large, finely chopped or pureed.
  • Capsicum (Green Bell Pepper): 1 small, finely chopped.
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tablespoon.
  • Green Chillies: 1-2, finely chopped (optional)
  • Amul Butter: At least 4-5 tablespoons (be generous!).
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon to prevent the butter from burning.
  • Ladi Pav: 1-2 packets of soft, fresh Indian bread rolls.

Step-by-step points to make Pav Bhaji:

  1. Boil & Mash the vegetables.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: take oil, saute onions, ginger-garlic paste and green chillies.
  3. Spice the Base: add tomatoes, add spices and powder, mix them properly.
  4. Simmer the Bhaji: mix mashed vegetables and base, add water, let it simmer.
  5. Toast the Pav: spread butter on pav and toast them on a pan.
